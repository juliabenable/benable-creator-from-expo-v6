Hyatt Regency Grand Cypress

The Hyatt Regency Grand Cypress lives up to its name: grand. As I stepped onto the grounds encompassing this magical hotel, I felt as though I had been plucked from Earth and catapulted straight into the artistic pages of a fantasy-themed storybook. The 800,000-gallon lagoon-style pool, twisting and turning through a maze of tunnels, lazy rivers, hot spring-style hot tubs, and waterslides, left me awe-struck. It was truly the most magnificent water park I have ever seen. But my amazement didn’t cease at the water’s edge. I uncovered a huge white sand beach, complete with plush lounge chairs, hammocks, and gentle waves kissing the shoreline—yes, in the middle of Orlando (for those unfamiliar, the ocean is hours away from this area). The hotel even provides its own lake for water sports, kayaking, canoeing, paddleboarding, fishing, and boat transportation to the Disney parks. I also discovered bike rentals, incredible cabanas, poolside bars, a swim-up bar, an ultra-luxurious salon, and spa. And let’s not forget the BEST buffet-style breakfast I’ve ever tasted, without a doubt.
